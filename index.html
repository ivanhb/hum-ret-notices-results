Results of content/metadata analysis of the retraction notices in HUM

Here you can find the results obtained using MITAO (https://github.com/catarsi/mitao):

  • LDAvis, a dynamic visualization showing the generated topics obtained after the application of a topic modeling analysis over the content of the retraction notices
  • MTMvis, a dynamic visualization showing the metadata of the retraction notices as a function of the topics generated after a topic modeling analysis
